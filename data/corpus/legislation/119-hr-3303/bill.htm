                                 A BILL


 
To require an exception to the license requirements for the exportation 
   of certain advanced integrated circuits to Israel, and for other 
                               purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``America--Israel AI Cooperation 
Act''.

SEC. 2. DIRECTED RULEMAKING WITH RESPECT TO ADVANCED INTEGRATED CIRCUIT 
              EXPORTATION TO ISRAEL.

    Notwithstanding any other provision of law, in finalizing the 
interim rule titled ``Framework for Artificial Intelligence Diffusion'' 
(90 Fed. Reg. 4544) the Secretary of Commerce, acting through the 
Undersecretary of Commerce for Industry and Security, shall modify the 
rule to--
            (1) include Israel in the list of countries set forth in 
        the amendment made by such rule to Supplement No. 5 to part 740 
        of title 15, Code of Federal Regulations; and
            (2) otherwise ensure that exports to Israel of the items 
        controlled pursuant to such rule are subject to the same 
        requirements and exceptions as exports to any of the countries 
        otherwise included in such supplement pursuant to the amendment 
        made by such rule.
