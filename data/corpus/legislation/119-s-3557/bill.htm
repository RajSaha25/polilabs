                                 A BILL


 
 To prohibit the use of Federal funds to implement the Executive order 
    entitled ``Ensuring a National Policy Framework for Artificial 
                            Intelligence''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``States' Right to Regulate AI Act''.

SEC. 2. NO FEDERAL FUNDS FOR EXECUTIVE ORDER RELATING TO STATE 
              REGULATION OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE.

    No Federal funds may be used to implement, administer, or enforce 
the Executive order entitled ``Ensuring a National Policy Framework for 
Artificial Intelligence'', issued on December 11, 2025.
